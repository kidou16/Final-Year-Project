Pomegranate Disease Detection

A machine learning-powered tool to help you identify diseases in your pomegranates.

Get Started
About The Project

Automation is playing an important role in day to day life. In India more than half the population depends upon agriculture. Exporting of fresh fruit is increased day to day from India. People are very conscious about their health; they prefer only fresh, good quality fruit.

How It Works

Our system uses a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) to analyze images of pomegranates and detect diseases like bacterial blight, butterfly pomegranate, and bitter rot. Simply upload an image and get instant results and preventive measures.
